Foodmux is built with love from these foodies!

Bharadwaj Giridhar.

Bharadwaj Giridhar is the founder and principal content creator of Foodmux. He created foodmux in 2017, after finding some cool restaurants in Madurai India, with no channels at that time covering these restaurants. With little help from his friends he was able to setup the channel and cover videos across Tamil Nadu. He is now amping up the channel to an app where people can share their love for food.
